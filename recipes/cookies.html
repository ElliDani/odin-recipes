Google Butter Cookies

Description

These cookies are rich, and buttery and will be sure to please!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup European-Style salted butter, room temperature
  • 8 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a stand mixer, beat together butter, cream cheese, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. With the mixer running, add in the egg and then the almond extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, stir together the flour, baking powder, and 1/4 cup of powdered sugar.
  5. With the mixer on low, slowly add the dry flour mixture to the already creamed mixture.
  6. Once smooth, remove the bowl from the mixer and cover. Place in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to chill.
  7. Line a large sheet pan with nonstick parchment paper. Place extra powdered sugar (for rolling) in a shallow bowl.
  8. Once chilled, use a 2 tablespoons cookie scoop to create cookie dough ball, immediately rolling fully in powdered sugar.
  9. Place on the baking sheet, 6 at a time, and bake for 10-11 minutes. When they are no longer wet on top and are firm o the sides, they are done. We prefer them on the shorter cook time so they are extra gooey!
  10. Allow to cool o the sheet pan for 5 minutes, and then place on a wire rack to cool completely.
